Services

Education planning with a clear strategy.

Services include school admissions, university applications, academic strategy, STEAM development, student-athlete planning, and support for gifted, high-potential, and neurodiverse learners.

Academic Strategy

Build a clear long-term academic and personal growth pathway. We help families understand the student’s strengths, academic profile, personality, interests, and future goals, then design a practical education roadmap.

  • School selection and pathway planning
  • Course planning and academic direction
  • Extracurricular and enrichment strategy
  • Family decision-making support

School Admissions

Personalized guidance for private school, boarding school... We support families through school research, student positioning, application planning, interviews, essays, parent communication, and final school decision-making.

  • School list strategy
  • Student profile positioning
  • Interview and application preparation
  • Parent communication and decision support

Elite School Visit, Interview & Family Readiness Program

Private school visits, interview preparation, school communication, and family decision support We support families exploring private schools, boarding schools, and international education pathways through in-depth school visit planning, interview preparation, school communication, and family decision support. This service may include school shortlisting, campus visit planning, student and parent interview preparation, first-round interview coaching, post-visit strategy review, and coordination of vacation programs, short-term stays, or student care when needed.

  • Elite school visit and itinerary planning
  • Student and parent interview preparation
  • First-round interview coaching and mock interviews
  • School communication and pre-visit preparation
  • Post-visit school fit and decision review
  • Vacation camp, short-term stay, and student care coordination

University Applications

Strategic planning for university applications, essays, activities, and academic positioning. We help students build a strong application narrative based on academic performance, interests, extracurricular activities, leadership, personal story, and future direction.

  • Major and university fit strategy
  • Activity and leadership development
  • Essay and application narrative planning
  • Scholarship and financial aid strategy

TOEFL & IELTS Language Training

We support students with TOEFL and IELTS preparation as part of their university application planning, helping them strengthen English proficiency for admissions and academic readiness.

  • English proficiency test preparation
  • Reading, writing, listening, and speaking support
  • Application-focused language improvement
  • Study planning for international students

GPA Management

Academic performance planning for stronger grades and healthier study routines. We help students identify academic gaps, improve study systems, plan course choices, manage workload, and build stronger communication with school and teachers.

  • Course selection and workload planning
  • Study routine and time management
  • Grade improvement strategy
  • School communication support

STEAM Development

Build interdisciplinary strength through science, technology, engineering, arts, and mathematics. We help students develop project-based experiences, competition pathways, research interests, robotics exposure, creative technology skills, and long-term STEM identity.

  • Robotics and engineering exposure
  • Competition and project planning
  • Research and innovation development
  • STEM identity building for applications

Student-Athlete Planning

Support for students balancing academics, athletic development, and admissions goals. We help student-athletes plan school fit, academic expectations, training schedules, athletic profiles, communication strategy, and long-term development pathways.

  • Academic and athletic balance
  • School and team fit strategy
  • Athletic profile development
  • Long-term training and admissions planning

Gifted, High-Potential & Neurodiverse Learner Support

Supportive education planning for gifted learners and students with diverse learning profiles. We help families understand a student’s learning profile, emotional needs, strengths, challenges, school support options, enrichment opportunities, and advocacy needs.

  • Strength-based student profile review
  • School support and accommodation guidance
  • Emotional and learning routine support
  • Family advocacy and communication strategy

Student Mental Health Support

Helping students build emotional stability while navigating academic pressure and school life

  • Academic stress and emotional well-being observation
  • School adjustment and peer relationship support
  • Family communication and parent-child relationship guidance
  • Referral suggestions for psychoeducational assessment or professional support when needed

Short-Term Guardianship & Student Care Support

Stable and caring support for students when parents are temporarily away We support families when parents are temporarily outside Canada, traveling, returning home, or unable to manage student matters in person. Our short-term guardianship and student care support may include school communication, emergency contact support, daily care coordination, regular family updates, and assistance with school, medical, or accommodation-related matters when needed.

  • Short-term guardianship arrangement
  • Communication with schools and teachers
  • Emergency contact and accompaniment support
  • Daily student care coordination
  • Regular updates while parents are away
  • Support with school, medical, or accommodation-related matters when needed
